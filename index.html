The current rates for 2021 are approximately $50 for smaller yards (roughly 3k-5k sqft). However these prices are subject to change and are determined by lot size, shape, and items in the yard.

We charge $0.40 per square foot. This can be as low as $200 if you only have a few square yards of leaves, or up to $1,000 if you have a large lot that's roughly 2,500 square feet. Leaf removal also requries a hauling fee if we are hauling bagged leaves off premises as opposed to blowing. (Note: we do have a minimum charge of $150 no matter the lot size). Currently we do not offer leaf removal from gutters.

While we understand having unsightly objects clutter your yard is something you want to get rid of quickly, we do want you to know that we are a lawncare business as opposed to a junk removal and hauling business. As such, we charge $100 per item to be hauled off so long as it's reasonable (reasonable is somewhat relative, but think "washing machine" or "grill" as reasonable items.
